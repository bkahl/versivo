    CRM for Government

    Versivo, and its partners, have the experience and resources to deliver Microsoft Dynamics-based solutions to address a wide range of Constituent Relationship Management ("CRM") challenges. Our framework is flexible and scalable - giving government organizations confidence that Versivo and its partners can not only implement a solution that meets your requirements today, but will evolve with your organization for years to come. Our solutions can be deployed cost effectively as either premised-based solutions or as hosted services (SaaS).

    Versivo's CRM experience and expertise include:

    • Contact Center / 311
    • Grant Management
    • Task Management
    • Personnel Management
    • Recruiting
    • Constituent Interaction Management
    • Citizen Case Management
    • Field Inspection
    • Comprehensive Business Intelligence
