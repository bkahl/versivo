    Government

    Versivo serves as a trusted advisor and provider of technology related products and services to local, state and federal governments. We work closely with our clients to recommend and deliver results oriented solutions that help our public sector clients collaborate and share information within and across agencies/departments, improve operational efficiency by reducing costs and cycle times associated with processing information and improve services to key constituents.

    Our public service team provides expert professional services to our public sector clients in the following areas:

    • Enterprise Content Management;
    • Business Process Improvement;
    • Collaboration;
    • Digital Government;
    • Systems Integration;
    • Program and Project Management;
    • Requirements Analysis and General Consulting;
    • Security and Identity Management; and
    • Software Engineering.
